## ğŸ—ï¸ **Architecture & Rigor**

### **The Nagomi Cascade**
When you execute a forensic check, Nagomi initiates a 4-level validation protocol:
1.  **Registry Match**: Validates the DOI/PMID exists in authoritative databases.
2.  **Author Verification**: Cross-references the cited authors against the registered metadata.
3.  **Field Mismatch Check**: Detects if a DOI resolves to a journal outside the cited domain.
4.  **Anomaly Reporting**: Explicitly flags "Frankenstein" citations or future-dated confabulations.
